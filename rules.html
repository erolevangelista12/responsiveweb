Basic Rules of Chess

Chess is a two-player strategy board game played on an 8x8 grid called a chessboard.

The game involves pieces such as pawns, rooks, knights, bishops, queens, and kings, each with its unique moves and abilities.

The objective of chess is to checkmate the opponent's king, placing it under an inescapable threat.

Learn the rules of movement, capturing, castling, and other essential aspects to become a skilled chess player.
