Chess Glossary

Explore the terminology and jargon used in the game of chess.

Checkmate
A position in chess where the opponent's king is under attack and cannot escape capture. The game ends when a checkmate occurs.
En Passant
A special pawn capture that can occur when a pawn moves two squares from its starting position and lands next to an opponent's pawn.
Zugzwang
A situation in chess where a player is forced to make a move that weakens their position or leads to a disadvantage.
